Here are some of my study projects. I use HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. Click on each work to find out more.

Shopping cart

Shopping cart

A simple Christmas e-shop that uses cookies to store products in the shopping cart. The product page lists products with picture, name, price and the Add button. The cart page shows products added by the user. The product list is generated with Javascript from a product template via direct DOM manipulation.

Image gallery

Image gallery

This is a gallery of images of my home town. The default view shows a gallery of image thumbnails and titles. Clicking on a thumbnail opens the overlay that shows the image in full size. User can navigate back and forth through the gallery directly from the overlay without having to go back to the thumbnail gallery. Created with HTML5 (figure element), CSS and JavaScript.

Brainstorm app

Brainstorm application

This is a simple solution that allows user to post ideas and discard them. A click on a suggested idea moves it to rejected and the other way around.
